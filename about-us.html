Understanding your vision

We understand your vision

A technical team member meets you to understand your vision for the product that you want to develop. This helps us clearly identify your needs and make appropriate proposals.

Statement of work (SoW)

We go away and work our heads down for a couple of days maximum a week for big projects. We provide you a document that lays out the entire plan for delivering your vision. This includes

  1. Goals
  2. Deliverables that we need from you and when do we need it.
  3. Our plan and Technologies used.
  4. Effort Estimate
  5. Deliverables and Milestones
  6. Acceptance criteria
  7. Payment Plan

The SoW is reviewed, corrected and agreed upon.

Design

Charged only on acceptance

LTypically for Smartphone applications this phase involves a UX design. You will be given frequent deliverable to get your feedback continuously. At the end of this phase you will be give a UX design with flow diagram.

For other Embedded or IoT solution this phase will produce an Architecture and design document.

Develop, test & deliver

Develop, test & deliver every two weeks : Charged only on acceptance

We are big time Agile fans. We work in 2 weeks sprint and produce something of value that can be demonstrated every 2 weeks. You get to see your vision reaching a reality every two weeks or more if you want to. You get a chance to provide continuous feedback and also monitor progress. You pay as you go.

Final acceptance

Deploy : Final acceptance

We complete the development, testing and on your acceptance we deploy the solution to App Stores, cloud or resources.
